What does it cost?

In most cases if you are covered for glass damage under your car insurance we will be able to repair the damage FREE of charge. To save you the hassle we can usually work directly with your insurer and manage the claim on your behalf, taking the stress out of claiming.

Act Before the Chip Becomes a Crack!

Windscreen chip repair cost less than replacing the crack. So, call us as soon as you notice the chip in your glass before it turns into a crack.

If your Windscreen does need replacing.

In some cases we will not be able to repair your vehicle glass and will therefore need to replace it. The cost of glass replacement is dependent on the make and model of your car. To find out how much replacement glass costs for your vehicle call us for a quick quote.

If you don't have Glass Damage Cover.

Don't worry if your insurance policy doesn't cover glass damage we can still repair or replace your vehicle glass affordably. Give is a call and we will discuss your options and give you a quote.
