Windscreen repair

Our 'Repair First Policy' at Windscreen Autoglaze Manchester means that we will always endeavour to repair your glass instead of replacing it unless you really need it.

How does it work?

Clean the area

Firstly our technicians will clean the damaged area and remove any dirt or debris which may be present

Inject Resin

A special epoxy resin is then injected into the damaged area.

Harden Resin

The resin will then be cured using UV light which will strengthen and protect returning your windscreen to full strength.

How long does it take to repair?

Dependent on the severity of the damage, our expert technicians can usually repair your vehicle glass damage within 30 minutes. Once we have completed the repair you can drive your vehicle immediately, minimising disruption to your day.

How well does it repair?

Once we have repaired the damaged area you may still notice a small mark left on the glass. However the repair will return your windscreen to full strength. Repairing your glass not only restores your windscreen strength, but makes it more aesthetically pleasing to see through, adds safer driving vision and improves the value of your car
